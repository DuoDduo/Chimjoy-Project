Who we are ?

A Topnotch Brand for you

Welcome to ChimJoy Car Hire, We are proud to be a subsidiary of Chimjoy Logistics Services Limited, a leading logistics firm operating in Owerri, Nigeria. At ChimJoy Car Hire, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between the untrusted high-priced car hire services and the numerous unorganized mediocre taxis that operate within Owerri Airport.

Our Focus

ChimJoy Car Hire is committed to being a positively responsive and, most importantly, safe road transport service in Nigeria. Whether you're headed to a special event, embarking on a corporate trip, shuttling between the airport and your hotel, or simply seeking a weekend getaway, we are here to serve you with utmost dedication.

Our mission

Our mission at ChimJoy Car Hire is to provide VIP-like service without the exorbitant prices typically associated with high-end limousines. While our vehicles may not be stretch limousines, they offer a great level of luxury, combined with affordability and reliability. We cater to the needs of both residents of Imo State and visitors from other states or countries, ensuring your comfort and mobility at Owerri Airport. Whether you're traveling alone or in a group, choose from our range of compact cars, sedans, SUVs, and comfortable mini-buses.
